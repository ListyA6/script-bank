## Voiceover — clean read

A photo studio was losing bookings in a group chat. So I built them one screen. They didn't need an app. They needed to stop double-booking the same slot. So I sat in their studio for a day. Watched how a booking actually happens. Front desk, the photographer, the client, all texting different threads. Turns out nobody owned the calendar. Three people could say yes to the same hour. So I didn't add features. I made one screen where a slot turns red the second it's taken. That's it. The whole build was two days. Because I wasn't guessing the problem from a brief. I saw it. That's the part a normal dev doesn't get to do. They haven't double-booked since. Asked me to do their invoices next.
