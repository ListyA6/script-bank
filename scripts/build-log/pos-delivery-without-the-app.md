# POS delivery, without the app

> We added a delivery-order feature to our own POS. A voucher with a QR code goes in every bag, the code opens our own order page, and the next-day order lands in the same admin + kitchen flow we already run. Then we deliver it ourselves.

## Voiceover — clean read

I added delivery to my POS. So customers can order from us without the delivery app. The app takes a cut of every order, and the customer stays theirs. I don't even get their number. Now every bag that leaves the kitchen has a voucher inside. On it, a QR code. It opens our order page. But the QR code isn't the clever part. Next day they scan it and order the same food, straight from us. And that order lands in the same system. Admin sees it, the kitchen gets the ticket. No new app, nothing for the staff to learn. It's just another ticket on the rail, and one of our guys rides it over. First one came in this week. Two bowls, same street as the shop.
