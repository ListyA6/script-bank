## Voiceover — clean read

I shut one of my shops down in April. Four months open. Then I closed it. Not because it lost money. Because I opened it before I had the one person who runs a kitchen when I'm not there. So every problem came straight to my phone. Supplier's late, my phone. Cook quits, my phone. I was running it from a different city, which, yeah, that's on me. The main shop was fine the whole time, because that one has the guy. So I added it up. Closing was cheaper than keeping it on life support. Two cooks from the closed one moved to the main kitchen. They start Monday.
