## Voiceover — clean read

I used to make logos. Now I build the system behind the counter. I ran a design agency for years. Websites, branding, the whole thing. And I was good at it. But I kept noticing the same thing. A business would pay for a beautiful brand, and behind it, the operations were held together with a group chat and one tired manager. The logo wasn't the problem. So I started asking what actually slowed them down. It was never the design. It was the booking, the stock, the orders nobody could track. That's the part I actually wanted to fix. So I learned to build the thing under the brand, not just the brand. Now I sit with an operator, find the one bottleneck, and build for that. Three months in. Still figuring it out.
