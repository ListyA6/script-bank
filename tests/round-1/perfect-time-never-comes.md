# The perfect time to launch

> Listy keeps waiting for the "right moment" to ship — misses the 7am slot, pushes it to 8, then lunch, then tomorrow. The loop never breaks on its own.

## Hooks — write 5+, pick 1

1. I had a feature ready to ship. I waited three weeks for the right moment.
2. My brain has one move: wait for a better time. The better time never comes.
3. I've shipped things at 7am. I've also delayed them to "7am tomorrow" for two weeks straight.
4. Miss 7am, wait for 8. Miss 8, wait for lunch. Miss lunch, wait for tomorrow. It's looping.
5. There's no good time to ship. I know this. I still wait for one.
6. I kept a feature in draft for eleven days because "the timing wasn't right."

**Chosen hook:** Miss 7am, wait for 8. Miss 8, wait for lunch. Miss lunch, wait for tomorrow. It's looping.

## Voiceover — clean read

Miss 7am, wait for 8. Miss 8, wait for lunch. Miss lunch, wait for tomorrow. It's looping.

I do this with launches, with features, with posts. Anything I have to actually put out. I had one feature basically done for eleven days, not broken, just sitting, because I kept moving the slot.

And here's what I figured out. There's no slot. My brain just makes one up so I don't have to ship yet. It's delay with a schedule attached.

So I stopped looking for the slot.
