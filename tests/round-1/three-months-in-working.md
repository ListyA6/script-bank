# Three months in. Is it working?

> Premise: Three months into building software full-time. When people ask if it's working yet, the honest answer is "I don't know yet" — but the client work is real even though the views are flat.

## Hooks — write 5+, pick 1
1. Someone asked me last week if the software thing is working yet. I didn't know what to say.
2. Three months into this and I still don't know if it's working. But here's what I do know.
3. I've been building software for clients for three months. Nobody's watching the videos. The clients are real.
4. People keep asking when this becomes a real business. I think it already is. I'm just not sure yet.
5. Three months of building in public and the views are still flat. So either I'm early or I'm wrong.
6. I don't have a clean answer for "is it working" yet. But the last client paid, and that feels like something.

**Chosen hook:** Three months into this and I still don't know if it's working. But here's what I do know.

## Voiceover — clean read
<!-- The full spoken track, exactly the way it's said. Read it aloud once. -->

Three months into this and I still don't know if it's working. But here's what I do know.

I left the agency work to build software full-time. Custom stuff — POS, booking, inventory — for businesses that already have the problem and need the fix.

The channel is basically flat. Views are what they are. But I've closed three client builds in these three months, and all of them came from someone who saw the work somewhere.

So here's the part I can't figure out.

Is that good? I honestly don't know. It's not what I pictured at month three. But I pictured the views metric and not the client metric, which is kind of a weird thing to admit. The restaurant took two years before it felt like a thing. So maybe I'm just impatient.

But when someone asks me "is it working yet," I used to say I don't know. Now I say: the build side is real, the audience side isn't there yet.

Three actual clients. Real workflows. One of them is running their whole booking system off something I made in five weeks. That part's working.

The views thing I'll figure out later. Or I won't. Either way I've got another build starting Monday.
