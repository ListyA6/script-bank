## Voiceover — clean read
<!-- The full spoken track, exactly the way it's said. Read it aloud once. -->

Friday night I had a blank repo. Sunday night the barbershop had a working booking system.

The owner runs three chairs. His problem was no-shows and double-bookings — walk-ins were just luck, whoever showed up first. So Saturday I built the appointment side first. Slots per barber, customer picks a time, they get a WhatsApp reminder two hours before.

But the walk-in part is where it got messy.

Walk-ins don't book a slot, so I added a live queue. Customer scans a QR at the door, joins the line, gets a message when there's one person ahead. The barber sees it on a tablet next to the chair.

I sent him the link Sunday evening. He tested it with his staff for about an hour. Monday they ran the whole day on it. Fourteen appointments, zero double-books, and the walk-in queue had six people in it by noon.

He said the only thing he wants to change is the font.
