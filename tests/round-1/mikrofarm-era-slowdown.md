## Voiceover — clean read
<!-- The full spoken track, exactly the way it's said. Read it aloud once. -->

I know exactly when I got slow again. It was a Tuesday. I reinstalled the game.

A few months back we had what I call the MikroFarm era. Me and the team were shipping every week, sometimes twice a week. New features, client builds, internal tools, all of it moving. I think I even surprised myself because I don't always run that way.

But I can point to the week it changed.

We finished a big delivery and I told myself I'd take a weekend off. And I did. And then Monday I opened the game again, just for an hour, and that hour kind of became the default.

I didn't notice for almost three weeks. Three weeks.

The team was still moving, the work was still there, but I wasn't pulling my weight on it. And the weird part is the output from that stretch looks fine from the outside. But I know the difference.

So now the game's uninstalled again. And I've got a rule: no reinstall until the week's work is done. Every week. We'll see.
