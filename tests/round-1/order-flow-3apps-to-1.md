# Order flow — 3 apps to 1 screen

> A client ran their entire order flow across three apps that didn't talk to each other. He replaced all of it with one screen.

## Hooks — write 5+, pick 1

1. My client was running their orders across three apps. None of them talked to each other.
2. Three apps, one order. That's how my client was running their kitchen.
3. I asked the owner how they track orders. She pulled out her phone and showed me three apps.
4. The order comes in on one app. The kitchen sees it on another. Billing is a third. Nobody built a fix for this because nobody here is a developer.
5. Before: three apps, one order, zero sync. After: one screen. Here's what it took.
6. She had GoFood open, WhatsApp open, and a spreadsheet open — all at the same time, every shift.

**Chosen hook:** She had GoFood open, WhatsApp open, and a spreadsheet open — all at the same time, every shift.
